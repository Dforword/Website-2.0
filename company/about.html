Our
story
Strategic discipline meets hands-on delivery

Ragu was founded over three years ago, when generative AI was emerging and enterprises were either on the sidelines or testing fragmented experiments. We saw something different: an opportunity to bring strategic discipline to AI adoption, pairing advisory experience with hands-on technical delivery.

We started as a platform company building unique, custom AI assistants. We evolved into something more useful - strategic advisors who also build. Today, we work with clients who share our belief that AI should elevate service, not erode it.

Capabilities
Our focus
High-touch businesses

While we are industry agnostic, our clients are hyperfocused on high-touch and luxury businesses. Engagements span luxury travel, fine wine and spirits, technical, financial, manufacturing and other sectors where judgment and nuance define the brand.

We understand the unique challenges of these environments - where service quality is non-negotiable, where relationships matter more than transactions, where getting it wrong carries real consequences.

Our AI solutions are designed with this context in mind. Not to replace the human judgment that makes these businesses special, but to amplify it.

What guides us
People first
We believe in the power of data to drive informed decisions. Our strategies are rooted in comprehensive analysis and insights, ensuring that every recommendation we make is backed by solid evidence and tailored to our clients' unique needs.
Craft over speed
We view business growth as a multifaceted journey that requires a holistic approach. By integrating market analysis, operational efficiency, and strategic planning, we provide solutions that address the full spectrum of our clients' challenges and opportunities.
Clarity before action
The hardest part of AI isn't the technology - it's knowing where to begin. We help leaders see the path before they commit to it.
A word from our CEO, Robertson Price
"We've never been interested in replacing the person in the chair; we're interested in giving them a better vantage point. Ragu AI is about ending the guesswork of 'generic' AI and replacing it with grounded intelligence—tools that actually know your business.

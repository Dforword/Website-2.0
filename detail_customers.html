The Feelings Engine

Customer
Black Tomato
Industry
Luxury Travel
Location
London, UK

Black Tomato creates bespoke, experiential journeys for discerning travelers. Their challenge: how do you truly understand what a traveler wants from their experience?

Traditional approaches rely on demographics, past bookings and stated preferences. But the most meaningful travel experiences connect at an emotional level - they fulfill needs that travelers often can't articulate themselves.

Black Tomato needed a way to understand their clients more deeply, to match them with experiences that would truly resonate.

The solution

Ragu built The Feelings Engine on our AI platform - a solution designed specifically for Black Tomato's approach to luxury travel.

How it works

Emotional understanding

AI that goes beyond demographics to understand what travelers actually want from their experiences.

Personalized prompts

Over 6,500 custom prompts designed to uncover preferences and match travelers with perfect experiences.

Intuitive matching

Recommendations based on emotional resonance, not just destination attributes or price points.

Human-centered design

Technology that enhances the work of Black Tomato's expert travel designers, not replaces it.

Measurable impact

13%

Higher conversion rate - travelers matched with the right experiences convert more often.
